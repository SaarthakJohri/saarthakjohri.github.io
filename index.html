Regardless of your path here, I'd like to welcome you to my personal digital billboard and present my academic & professional story --- with the hope it will be more holistic and cohesive than I can present in a resume and cover letter.

While I initially majored in physics and approached truth from a scientific angle, my academic and professional opportunities allowed me to seek these truths hiding in our cosmos, history, and arts --- all while honing skills to best communicate these truths. At the end of my undergraduate studies and current employment, I am now seeking positions that will allow me to use my scientific, journalistic, pedagogical background and tools to improve the world with the truths we uncover and share.
