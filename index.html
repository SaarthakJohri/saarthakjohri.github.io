About Me

Hi! My name is Saarthak. For my entire life, I have sought fundamental truths about our world. This quest led me to starting my studies at the University of Michigan. While I initially majored in physics and approached truth from a scientific angle, my academic and professional opportunities allowed me to seek these truths hiding in our cosmos, history, and arts --- all while honing skills to best communicate these truths. At the end of my undergraduate studies and current employment, I am now seeking positions that will allow me to use my scientific, journalistic, pedagogical background and tools to improve the world with the truths we uncover and share.
